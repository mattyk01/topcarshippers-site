§ 01Methodology

How we pick the best car shippers.

Not every auto transport company is worth your time. We evaluate each one on six criteria: pricing transparency, carrier vetting practices, customer communication, delivery performance, complaint resolution, and overall value. Companies that play games with quotes or hide fees don’t make the list. The ones that do earn their spot.

01Criterion

Pricing transparency

The quote you get should be the price you pay. We flag companies that lowball to win the booking and adjust at pickup.

02Criterion

Carrier vetting

Brokers are only as good as the carriers they dispatch. We look at how companies screen drivers and handle damage claims.

03Criterion

Delivery performance

On-time pickups, accurate windows, vehicles arriving in the condition they left. This is the whole point.
