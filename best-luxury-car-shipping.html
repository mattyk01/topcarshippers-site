Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about luxury vehicle shipping

Do I need enclosed transport for a luxury vehicle?

For most high-value vehicles, enclosed is the right choice. It protects against weather, road debris, and visibility during transit. The premium over open carrier is typically $200 to $500 for most routes and is worth it for vehicles where cosmetic condition matters.

What should I look for when shipping a luxury or classic car?

Carrier vetting is the most important factor. Ask how the company screens the drivers they dispatch. Photo documentation at pickup and delivery, enclosed transport availability, and verifiable insurance coverage are the other key criteria.

How much does it cost to ship a luxury vehicle?

Enclosed transport for a luxury vehicle typically runs $1,000 to $2,500 for most domestic routes depending on distance, vehicle size, and timing. Exotic or oversized vehicles may cost more.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

All FMCSA-registered carriers are required to carry cargo insurance. However, coverage limits and deductibles vary by carrier. Ask about the specific per-vehicle limit and whether supplemental coverage is available if your vehicle's value exceeds the carrier's policy limit.
