1. RoadRunner Auto Transport Best Overall
★★★★★ 4.9 / 5
Top-rated car shipping company for 2026

RoadRunner has been shipping cars for 30 years and that operational history shows. They run a proprietary load board and an in-house team of transport coordinators, dispatch specialists, and shipment support agents who stay with your order from booking through delivery. That is not how most brokers operate. Most brokers post your vehicle to a shared marketplace and wait for a carrier to bite. RoadRunner actively matches carriers from their vetted network, which means fewer no-shows and more consistent pickup windows.

Pricing is transparent upfront with no lowball quote followed by a surprise add-on at pickup. Unlike most competitors, RoadRunner requires no deposit at booking; you pay only when a carrier is confirmed. They offer both open and enclosed transport, door-to-door delivery in most markets, and real-time tracking. For the majority of standard shipments, RoadRunner offers the best combination of price, reliability, and customer accountability we have seen in this industry.
