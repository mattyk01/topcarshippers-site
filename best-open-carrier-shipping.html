Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about open carrier shipping

What is open carrier car shipping?

Open carrier shipping transports your vehicle on an open multi-car trailer. It is the most common method used in the industry. Your vehicle is exposed to weather and road conditions during transit, which is generally not an issue for standard vehicles.

Is open carrier safe?

Yes, for the vast majority of shipments. Open carrier is how manufacturers move new cars from factories to dealerships. Road debris and weather exposure are the main risks, but damage rates are low across reputable carriers.

How much does open carrier shipping cost?

Open carrier typically costs between $500 and $1,500 for most routes. Cross-country routes run $900 to $1,400 on average. Enclosed transport costs $200 to $500 more for the same route.

How long does open carrier shipping take?

Most domestic shipments take 1 to 2 weeks. Cross-country moves typically take 7 to 10 days. Expedited options are available from most brokers for an additional fee.
